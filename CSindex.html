Counter-Strike 2   

Developer: Valve

System Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: 4 hardware threads – Intel® Core™ i5-750 or equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Video card with 1 GB VRAM or more, compatible with DirectX 11 and Shader Model 5.0
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: ~85 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-9700 / AMD Ryzen 7 2700X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 / AMD Radeon RX 5700 XT or better
  • DirectX: Version 11 (12 for best effects)
  • Storage: ~85 GB available space

For more than two decades, Counter-Strike has served as an example of a first-class competitive game, whose development path is determined by millions of players from all over the world. Now it's time for a new stage — Counter-Strike 2.

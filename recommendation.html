Recommended Games

RPG

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an award-winning open-world RPG developed by CD Projekt RED. Players follow Geralt of Rivia, a professional monster hunter, as he embarks on a perilous quest to find his adopted daughter while facing powerful foes, political intrigue, and moral choices that shape the story’s outcome.

Indie

Hollow Knight: Silksong

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the highly anticipated sequel to Hollow Knight, developed by Team Cherry. Players take on the role of Hornet, a swift and agile warrior, as she journeys through a vast, haunted kingdom filled with deadly enemies, intricate platforms, and mysterious secrets to uncover.

Sandbox

Terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox adventure game developed by Re-Logic. It combines exploration, building, crafting, combat, and survival in a procedurally generated world filled with diverse biomes, dangerous creatures, and countless treasures to discover.

Adventure RPG

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Special Edition

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Special Edition is a remastered version of the iconic open-world RPG developed by Bethesda Game Studios. It offers enhanced graphics, all DLC expansions, allowing players to explore the vast province of Skyrim, and shape their destiny as the legendary Dragonborn.

Action

Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. Set in the sprawling city of Los Santos and its surrounding areas, the game follows three unique protagonists as they navigate a world of crime, heists, and chaos, offering players freedom to explore, complete missions, and experience an expansive online mode.

Life Simulator

Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is a charming farming simulation RPG developed by ConcernedApe. Players inherit a rundown farm in a quiet village and work to restore it by growing crops, raising animals, mining, fishing, and building relationships with the local townsfolk in a relaxing, open-ended world.

Psychological Horror

SILENT HILL f

SILENT HILL f is a new entry in the iconic horror series developed by NeoBards Entertainment and published by Konami. Set in 1960s Japan, it combines a haunting, atmospheric story with psychological horror, exploring dark secrets and eerie supernatural phenomena in a uniquely unsettling setting.

Sport Simulation

EA SPORTS FC™ 26

EA SPORTS FC™ 26 is the latest football simulation game from EA, featuring over 20,000 players, 750+ clubs, and 35 leagues. It offers improved gameplay, realistic animations, and new modes for an authentic football experience.

MOBA

Dota 2

Dota 2 is a free-to-play multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Valve. Two teams of five players battle to destroy the enemy’s Ancient, using a diverse roster of heroes with unique abilities and strategies.

Dying Light: The Beast — a thrilling open-world survival horror set in a city consumed by the infected. By day, scavenge for weapons and craft powerful gear, explore a crumbling urban landscape, and face dangerous human factions. By night, the hunters become the hunted — terrifying predators lurk in the shadows, stronger and faster than ever. Use parkour, strategy, and your will to survive in a world where every step could be your last.
